# Call Recording Disclosure

## Module 0 opener
Every call begins with: "Thanks for calling Government Vacation Rewards. This call may be recorded for quality."

## Two-party consent states
California (CA), Washington (WA), Hawaii (HI), and Florida (FL) require all-party consent. The Module 0 opener satisfies disclosure for these states.

## Why
TCPA + state two-party consent statutes. Disclosure must be at the start of the interaction, not buried in terms.
