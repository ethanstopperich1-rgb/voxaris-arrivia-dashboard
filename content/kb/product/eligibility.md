# Membership Eligibility

## Who qualifies
Membership is open to:
- Active-duty U.S. military
- Retired U.S. military
- Federal civilian employees
- First responders (law enforcement, fire, EMS)
- Immediate family of any of the above (spouses, dependents)

## Verification
Eligibility verification is handled by a licensed specialist after transfer. The voice agent does not validate eligibility documents.
