# Eligible Travel

## Categories
Eligible travel includes hotels, resorts, cruises, travel packages, and select activities booked through the GVR member portal.

## Cruises
Major cruise lines are part of GVR's negotiated inventory. Specific itineraries, cabin classes, and sailings vary by season.

## Resorts
GVR partners with major resort brands. Availability and rates vary by destination and date.

## Hotels
Negotiated hotel rates are available across major chains and select independent properties.

## Blackout dates
Blackout dates and seasonal availability vary by destination. A specialist can pull live availability for the dates you're considering.
