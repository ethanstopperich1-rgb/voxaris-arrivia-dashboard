# Travel Savings Dollars

## Definition
Travel savings dollars are promotional savings that may be applied toward eligible travel purchases through GVR, subject to offer terms.

## Not cash
Travel savings dollars are not redeemable as cash and are not a general cash balance. They cannot be withdrawn, transferred to a bank account, or used outside the GVR member portal.

## Different from a gift card
A gift card carries a stated balance you can spend at participating merchants. Travel savings dollars are promotional savings that apply only at booking, only on eligible travel categories, only through the GVR member portal.

## Different from cash back
Cash back posts to your account or statement. Travel savings dollars apply at booking against eligible travel through GVR.

## Expiration
Expiration windows vary by offer. A licensed specialist can confirm exact dates tied to your account — the agent will not quote expiration over the phone.
