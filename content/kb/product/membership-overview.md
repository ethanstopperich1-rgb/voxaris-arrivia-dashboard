# Membership Overview

## Tiers
GVR offers a tiered membership structure. The voice agent does not quote tier pricing or point allocations — these are confirmed by a licensed specialist on transfer.

## Member portal
Members access negotiated rates through the GVR member portal, which handles authentication, eligible-travel browsing, and booking workflow.

## Member services
Member services is available seven days a week. Account-specific lookups, booking changes, and pricing questions are routed to a verified specialist for the member's security.

## Mobile / app
GVR provides web-based access to the member portal. App availability and feature parity should be confirmed with a specialist.
