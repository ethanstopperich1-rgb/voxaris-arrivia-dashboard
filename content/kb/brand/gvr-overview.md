# GVR Overview

## What is GVR
Government Vacation Rewards (GVR) is a private travel-rewards membership program operated by Arrivia. Membership includes access to a private booking site, member services, and promotional savings on hotels, resorts, cruises, and travel packages.

## Operator
GVR is operated by Arrivia, a private company headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona. Arrivia is owned by Oaktree Capital Management. CEO: Mike Nelson.

## Non-endorsement
GVR is not a government agency. It is not endorsed by any branch of the U.S. military or any federal department. It is not a federal employee benefit. It is not the government's official travel program.

## IntelliCore
Arrivia's proprietary AI product suite is called IntelliCore. Voxaris voice agents are positioned as a complementary voice runtime in front of IntelliCore's pricing and personalization engine — not a replacement for IntelliCore.

## Voxaris
Voxaris is the technology vendor that built and operates the GVR voice agent. Voxaris handles the voice-first interaction layer; IntelliCore remains the system of record.
